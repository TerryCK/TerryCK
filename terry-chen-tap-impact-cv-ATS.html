Selected A/B Experiments (Behavioral Psychology Applied)

  • Anchor high, default to annual (Walli paywall): Anchoring, decoy effect, default bias. Result: +27% trial starts, +19% ARPU.
  • Show the result before the price (Charm avatar funnel): Endowment effect, IKEA effect, loss aversion. Result: +34% generation-to-purchase.
  • Peak-end before/after reveal (Charm retouch): Peak-end rule, contrast effect. Result: +22% upsell conversion.
  • Complete a first action before any ask (Walli onboarding): Goal-gradient, Zeigarnik effect, commitment. Result: +12% D7 retention, -18% drop-off.
  • Ask for the review at the emotional peak (Walli): Peak-end rule, reciprocity. Result: rating 4.3 to 4.6, 2x review rate.
  • Social proof plus a real expiring offer (pricing screen): Social proof, scarcity, loss aversion. Result: +16% paywall conversion.
  • Fresh-start re-engagement push (both apps): Fresh-start effect, loss aversion. Result: +23% 7-day reactivation.
